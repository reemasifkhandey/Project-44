They come across various obstacles and hindrances such as meteoroids trying to destroy the spaceship, aliens in UFOs trying to harm the astronauts as they see them as their enemies. The spaceship will have a weapon installed in it to use it against the enemies. The astronauts have to protect themselves and their spaceship from the obstacles. The score will increase accordingly.
